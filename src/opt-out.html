How to opt out of emergency alerts

If you opt out, you will not get any local emergency alerts.

You cannot choose the type of hazard you want to get alerts about. If you opt out because you do not want flood warnings, you’ll miss alerts for fires and terrorist incidents as well.

Because of this, we recommend you keep emergency alerts switched on for your own safety.

Android phones and tablets

To opt out, search your settings for ‘emergency alerts’ and turn off Severe threats

If this does not work, contact your device manufacturer.

iPhone and iPad

To opt out, search your settings for ‘emergency alerts’.

If this does not work, contact your device manufacturer.
